Knights of Gargantua

Gargantua is a dangerous city, flailed by the Cult of the Veil. Corruption has rotten the flesh and now horrifying beings roam the streets of the city...

Knights of Gargantua is a simple browser game, letting you fight endlessly against hordes of random monsters. Each time you defeat a monster, a new one appears. Monster stats are randomly calculated based on your level.

Mind your Health Points, don't forget to enchant your weapons and don't forget to survive!
